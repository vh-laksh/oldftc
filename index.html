Who are Polaris Robotics?

Mission

Our mission is to inspire underprivileged children in our community to learn about robotics, and are working hard to make it happen!.

Team Polaris Robotics

Polaris Robotics is an extremely passionate group of high school students in Fremont, CA. Formed in 2020, Polaris Robotics has years of experience designing, building, and programming award-winning—and competition-winning robots!

Team Bio's
Goal for this year

This year, we want to go beyond everything we have done. We want to go into Worlds and known as a renowned team in the FIRST organization. To get to Worlds, we have set a strategized plan where we build the robot in a month and a half. From there, we test for at least 3 weeks. We hope to ace our Qualifier to get into Regionals. Once we are in Regionals, we try our hardest to make Worlds and compete with other top-notch teams.

Our Outreach

Polaris Robotics has given back to the community in numerous ways.

2 FLL Teams

We have mentored 2 FLL teams. Few of them moved into FTC this year and are part of a new created team which we are mentoring.

5 Explore FLL Teams

We wanted to reach a different group of aspirers to implement robotics. As numerous young children were interested, we were able to guide them through robotics for their first year.

Hosting Norcal FLL Tournament

Giving back to your community also means that we have to give back to FIRST. We have done so by organizing, hosting and volunteering some of our parents to be judges.

Classes

We offer a multitude of educational opportunities through tutorials that are crafted and delivered by students. We have created a few youtube videos for CAD. We also taught CAD, Scratch and Java to students outside of schools.

Work with Nearby Schools

During the summer, we worked with Harvey Green about robotics and guiding them about FIRST.

International Talks

We have reached all aspects of our community. From competition wise to the range of schools and teams. Now, we have reached out to the world. We have given speeches and talked about robotics and FIRST in Africa, India and numerous places.
